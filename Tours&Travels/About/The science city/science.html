Science City Siliguri is a modern science center and museum located in the city of Siliguri, in the Indian state of West Bengal. The center was established in 2018 with the aim of promoting science education and awareness among the general public, especially children and young people. Spread over an area of 20 acres, Science City Siliguri houses a range of exhibits and interactive displays that showcase various scientific principles and phenomena. The center features several galleries that cover topics such as physics, chemistry, biology, space science, and information technology. One of the highlights of Science City Siliguri is the Digital Planetarium, which uses advanced projection technology to provide an immersive experience of the night sky. Visitors can enjoy a simulated journey through the solar system, and learn about the constellations, planets, and other celestial bodies. Another popular exhibit at Science City Siliguri is the Science Park, which features a range of outdoor exhibits that demonstrate the principles of physics and mechanics. These include a giant pendulum, a musical fountain, a gravity well, and a human gyroscope. The center also has a dedicated section for children, called the Kids Zone, which features several interactive exhibits and games designed to stimulate their curiosity and interest in science. The exhibits in this section are designed to be fun and engaging, and help children learn about scientific concepts in a hands-on way. In addition to the exhibits, Science City Siliguri also hosts regular workshops, seminars, and events for students, teachers, and the general public.
