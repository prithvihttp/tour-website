Bengal Safari is a popular wildlife park located in Siliguri, West Bengal, India. Spread over an area of 297 hectares, it is a major tourist attraction in the region. The park is home to a wide variety of animals, including Royal Bengal tigers, Asiatic elephants, Indian rhinoceros, Himalayan black bears, and many more. It is managed by the West Bengal Forest Development Corporation (WBFDC) and was inaugurated by Chief Minister Mamata Banerjee in January 2016. The park is divided into two sections: the Tiger Safari and the Leopard Safari. The Tiger Safari is the main attraction of the park and is spread over an area of 30 hectares. Visitors can take a jeep safari and explore the dense forest to spot tigers in their natural habitat. The Leopard Safari is a similar experience, where visitors can observe leopards in their natural environment. Apart from the Tiger and Leopard safaris, the park also has a Bear Safari, where visitors can witness the playful antics of Himalayan black bears. The park also has an Elephant Safari, where visitors can take a ride on the gentle giants and explore the lush green surroundings. There is also a Primate Island, where visitors can observe various species of monkeys and apes. The park has a wide variety of flora and fauna, making it a paradise for nature lovers. The forest cover consists of Sal, Teak, and Eucalyptus trees, and the park is home to more than 100 species of birds, making it a popular destination for bird watchers. The park has a well-developed infrastructure to cater to the needs of tourists. There are several watchtowers and viewpoints from where visitors can get a panoramic view of the park. The park also has a cafeteria that serves snacks and refreshments. There is also a souvenir shop where visitors can buy mementos and gifts. The park has a strong focus on conservation and education. There is a museum on the premises that showcases the biodiversity of the region and highlights the importance of conservation. The park also conducts educational tours and camps for school children to create awareness about wildlife conservation. The Bengal Safari has been a boon for the local economy, generating employment opportunities for the people in the region. The park has also helped in the conservation of wildlife and has raised awareness about the importance of preserving natural habitats. In conclusion, the Bengal Safari in Siliguri is a must-visit destination for anyone who loves wildlife and nature. The park offers an immersive experience, where visitors can witness animals in their natural environment. With its well-developed infrastructure and focus on conservation, the Bengal Safari is a perfect example of responsible tourism
